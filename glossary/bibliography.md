# Bibliography & Further Reading

This page offers recommended books, reports, articles and other media for further exploration. It is a living list and you’re encouraged to contribute.

### Selected Titles

- Heumann, Judith. *Being Heumann: An Unrepentant Memoir of a Disability Rights Activist.* – An accessible first-person account of disability rights activism.  
- Ladau, Emily. *Demystifying Disability: What to Know, What to Say, and How to Be an Ally.* – A practical guide for allies and new learners.  
- Clare, Eli. *Exile and Pride: Disability, Queerness, and Liberation.* – Essays exploring intersections of disability, gender, race, and environment.  
- World Health Organization. “World Report on Disability.” – A global overview of disability prevalence, rights, and policy (2011).  
- W3C Web Accessibility Initiative. *Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1.* – The core standard for web accessibility globally.
