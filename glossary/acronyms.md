# Acronyms

This page provides definitions of acronyms commonly used in this wiki and in disability policy, law, access etc. Use this as a quick reference.

| Acronym | Full Term                           | Notes/Region            |
|---------|-------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| ADA     | Americans with Disabilities Act     | United States            |
| IEP     | Individualized Education Program    | U.S. K–12 education      |
| WCAG    | Web Content Accessibility Guidelines| Global standard          |
| SSI     | Supplemental Security Income        | United States            |
| NDIS    | National Disability Insurance Scheme| Australia                |
