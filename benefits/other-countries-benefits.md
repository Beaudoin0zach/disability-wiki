# Disability Benefits – Other Countries & Regions

This page is a **catch-all and starting point** for disability benefits information in countries and regions not yet covered by their own pages.

Many places:

- Have limited or fragile state benefits,  
- Rely heavily on family networks, community groups, and NGOs,  
- Are affected by colonization, conflict, austerity, or international financial pressures.
