# Money & Benefits

This section of the wiki is about **how disabled people survive and live** in systems that often make basic needs hard to meet. It focuses on:

- Income support and cash benefits  
- Health coverage and disability-related programs  
- Food, housing, and caregiving support  
- Debt, budgeting, and appeals when things go wrong  

> ⚠️ **Important:** Rules and amounts change often. This wiki is *not* legal advice. Always double-check with an official source (e.g., government websites) or a qualified advocate in your area.
