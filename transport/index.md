# Transportation & Mobility

Being able to move through the world—safely and affordably—is a core access issue.

This section includes:

- **Public Transit Rights** – Accessibility standards, complaints, and organizing.
- **Paratransit** – Eligibility, appeals, and common pitfalls.
- **Air Travel Rights** – Laws, complaint processes, and practical tips.
- **Driving and Adaptive Driving** – Licensing, vehicle modifications, and funding.
- **Mobility Aids** – Wheelchairs, scooters, canes, crutches, and how transit systems handle (or mishandle) them.
- **Service Animals** – Travel rules, documentation, and access fights.

Transportation connects directly to **housing, employment, healthcare, and community**. Failures here are policy choices, not personal shortcomings.
