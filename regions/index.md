# Regions & Country Guides

Disability laws, benefits, and services vary dramatically from place to place.

This section is for **country and regional guides**, including:

- Overviews of disability benefits and healthcare systems
- Key rights frameworks and enforcement bodies
- Major disability organizations and grassroots groups
- Region-specific crisis, housing, or legal resources

Planned structure (to be expanded):

- Country-level pages (for example, `/regions/canada`, `/regions/uk`, `/regions/australia`)
- Regional groupings where useful (for example, EU, West Africa, South Asia)
- Sub-pages for provinces, states, or territories where systems differ significantly

We especially welcome contributions about:

- The Global South
- Indigenous territories
- Regions under occupation or facing climate disasters

Each guide should clearly state **which jurisdiction(s)** it covers and when it was last updated.
