# Welcome to the Disability Wiki

This is a community resource **by and for disabled people**.

We center disability justice, intersectionality, and lived experience.

## What You'll Find Here

Practical, actionable resources on disability rights, benefits, housing, 
employment, health, education, and community support.

**Everything here is written by disabled people.**

## We're Accessible First

- Works with screen readers
- Keyboard navigable
- Mobile-friendly  
- Plain language
- Trauma-informed
