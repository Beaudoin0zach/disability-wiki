# Community & Connection

Isolation is one of the most common experiences disabled people name—especially early on.

This section gathers ways to find or build community:

- **Online Communities** – Forums, social media, Discord, and other digital spaces.
- **Disability-Specific Peer Groups** – By diagnosis, identity, or experience.
- **In-Person Community** – Local groups, centers for independent living, meetups.
- **Youth & Student Communities** – School- and campus-based spaces, youth-led groups.

Where possible, we highlight **disabled-run spaces** and note safety considerations (privacy, moderation, targeting by scammers or abusers).
