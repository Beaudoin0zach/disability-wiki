# Crisis & Safety

This section focuses on **immediate safety and urgent situations**—from suicidal thoughts to domestic violence, disasters, or sudden loss of income and housing.

> If you are in immediate danger, follow the emergency options available in your area.  
> This wiki cannot respond in real time.

---

## What you’ll find here

- **Global Crisis Hotlines** – International crisis lines, warmlines, text and chat options where available.
- **Disabled Crisis Support** – Crisis spaces and collectives that understand disability, chronic illness, neurodivergence, and mad experiences.
- **Abuse, Neglect, and Exploitation** – Recognizing and responding to violence in families, institutions, care settings, and workplaces.
- **Emergency & Disaster Preparedness** – Planning for fires, floods, storms, power outages, and evacuations when you or loved ones are disabled.

These pages try to name both **risks** (for example, policing, involuntary treatment, benefit sanctions) and **safer alternatives** where they exist.
