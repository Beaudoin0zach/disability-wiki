# Daily Living

This section gathers the **everyday stuff** that rarely shows up in policy documents but shapes our lives.

You’ll find pages on:

- **Assistive Technology Overview** – Tools that support communication, mobility, organization, and sensory regulation.
- **Personal Care** – Hygiene, dressing, toileting, and attendant care.
- **Cooking and Nutrition** – Energy-saving approaches, adaptive tools, and food access.
- **Exercise and Adaptive Sports** – Movement that respects disabled bodyminds.
- **Recreation, Travel, and Outdoors** – Hobbies, nature access, and trips.
- **Accessible Travel** – Planning routes, lodging, and supports.
- **Fashion, Beauty, and Adaptive Wear** – Clothing, gear, and expression.
- **Pets and Service Animals** – Companions, working animals, and legal distinctions.

These pages prioritize **practical hacks and lived experience** over perfect routines.
