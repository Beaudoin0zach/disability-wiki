# Healthcare & Access

Disabled people interact with healthcare systems more often—and with more risk—than many non-disabled people.

This section includes:

- **Accessible Healthcare** – Making appointments, physical access, communication access, and informed consent.
- **Healthcare Rights** – Legal protections against discrimination in care.
- **Insurance Navigation** – Understanding public and private insurance, appeals, and denials (see also Benefits).
- **Medical Equipment & Assistive Technology** – Getting, maintaining, and repairing devices.
- **Mental Health** – Accessing support while accounting for power, coercion, and cultural context.
- **Pain & Fatigue** – Chronic pain, energy limitations, pacing, and fatigue management.
- **Home & Community-Based Care** – Home care, attendants, family caregiving, and fights against unnecessary institutionalization.

We aim to be honest about **ableism in healthcare** while still offering practical strategies.
