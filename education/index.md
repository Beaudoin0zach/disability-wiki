# Education

This section looks at education as a **lifelong process**, not just school years.

Pages include:

- **Early Intervention** – Services for infants and toddlers, and support for families.
- **K-12 Education** – IEPs, 504 plans, accommodations, and school culture.
- **Higher Education** – Colleges, universities, trade schools, and access offices.
- **Adult & Continuing Education** – Community education, vocational training, and non-formal learning.
- **Transition to Adulthood** – Moving from school-based services into adult systems, benefits, and work.

We pay attention to how race, class, immigration status, and disability intersect to shape who is **pushed out, over-disciplined, or denied** education.
