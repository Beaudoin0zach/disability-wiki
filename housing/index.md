# Housing & Living Arrangements

Where and how we live shapes almost everything else.

This section includes:

- **Accessible Housing Search Guide** – Finding and evaluating housing, with and without formal accessibility codes.
- **Housing Rights** – Anti-discrimination protections, reasonable accommodations, and modifications.
- **Home Modifications** – Funding and practical tips for ramps, bathrooms, doorways, and more.
- **Tenants’ Rights with Disabilities** – Working with landlords, housing authorities, and co-ops.
- **Homelessness and Disability** – Barriers, shelters, encampments, and housing-first approaches.
- **Group Homes and Institutions** – Risks, histories, and alternatives.
- **Independent Living Philosophy and Centers** – Cross-disability approach to self-determination and community living.

See also **Benefits** (for housing subsidies) and **Crisis** (for emergencies and displacement).
