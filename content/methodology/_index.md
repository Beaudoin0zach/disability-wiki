## Purpose

This site documents publicly available statements and actions by New York's 23rd Congressional District representative using official government records and archived sources.

## Our Process

### 1. Source Collection
We monitor:
- Official congressional website press releases
- Official social media accounts
- Congress.gov voting records
- Floor statements and official remarks
- Established news sources

### 2. Verification Standards
Every entry on this site must meet these criteria:

- **Primary sources only**: We link directly to congress.gov, official websites, and archived pages
- **Complete context**: Full quotes with dates and circumstances
- **Archived evidence**: All sources archived via Archive.org for permanence
- **Factual accuracy**: Claims verified against multiple official sources
- **No speculation**: We document what was said and done, not motives or intentions

### 3. What We Document

**Statements**: Press releases, social media posts, newsletters, floor speeches, and public remarks

**Actions**: Congressional votes, bill sponsorships, committee participation

**Comparisons**: Side-by-side presentation of statements and corresponding actions

### 4. What We Don't Do

- Make accusations of criminal conduct
- Speculate about motives or intentions
- Present opinion as fact
- Use anonymous or unverifiable sources
- Selectively edit quotes to change meaning

## Corrections Policy

Accuracy is paramount. If we make an error:

1. We will correct it prominently and immediately
2. We will note the correction with a timestamp
3. We will explain what was incorrect and why

If you believe we've made an error, please contact us with documentation.

## Independence

This is an independent research project by constituents of NY-23. It is not affiliated with any political campaign, party organization, or advocacy group.

## Transparency

All sources are linked and archived. Readers can verify every claim independently.
