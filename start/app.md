# Get the App: Install Disability Wiki on Your Device

You can install this wiki on your phone, tablet, or computer today. It opens from your
home screen like any other app, and the crisis pages work **without a signal** — useful
in a waiting room with no reception, on a bus, or when your data runs out.

Installing is free. It does not create an account, and it does not ask for your name,
email, or phone number.

## Install it now

### iPhone or iPad (Safari)

1. Open [disabilitywiki.org](https://disabilitywiki.org) in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**.

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open [disabilitywiki.org](https://disabilitywiki.org) in Chrome.
2. Tap the **menu** button (three dots, top right).
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** or **Install app**.
4. Tap **Install**.

### Computer (Chrome or Edge)

1. Open [disabilitywiki.org](https://disabilitywiki.org).
2. Look for the **install icon** in the address bar (a screen with a down arrow).
3. Click it, then click **Install**.

If you do not see the option, your browser may not support installing. The site still
works normally in any browser — installing is a convenience, not a requirement.

## What works offline

Once installed, these work with no connection:

- All [crisis pages](/crisis), including hotline numbers
- The Spanish [crisis pages](/es/crisis)
- Pages you have already visited

Everything else needs a connection. When you *are* online, the app always fetches the
current version of a page first — so a hotline number that has changed will never be
served to you stale.

## A native app is in progress

We are building a native iOS app that bundles the whole wiki, so it works from the
moment you open it the first time — no connection needed even for the first load.

It is **not available yet**. There is no release date, and we will not promise one we
are not sure of. When it is ready, this page will say so.

There is no Android app yet either.

## Questions

- **Does it cost anything?** No. There is no paid version and no ads.
- **Does it track me?** No. The wiki has no analytics, no account, and no tracking.
- **Will it use a lot of storage?** No. The offline crisis pages are text and take up
  very little space.
- **How do I remove it?** Delete it like any other app — press and hold the icon, then
  choose Remove or Uninstall.
