# Contact Us

DisabilityWiki is built by and for disabled people. We welcome your questions, suggestions, corrections, and contributions.

-----

## Ways to Reach Us

### General Inquiries

**Email:** contact@disabilitywiki.org

Use this for:

- Questions about the wiki
- Partnership inquiries
- Media requests
- General feedback

### Content Suggestions & Corrections

**Email:** contribute@disabilitywiki.org

Use this for:

- Suggesting new topics or pages
- Reporting outdated or inaccurate information
- Offering to contribute content
- Accessibility issues with the site

### Report a Problem

**Email:** support@disabilitywiki.org

Use this for:

- Broken links or technical issues
- Accessibility barriers on the site
- Concerns about content

Want to help build this resource? See our [How to Contribute](/start/contribute) page for:

## Response Times

We’re a small team, mostly volunteers. We aim to respond within:

- **Urgent accessibility issues:** 24-48 hours
- **Content corrections:** 3-5 business days
- **General inquiries:** 1-2 weeks

If your message is time-sensitive, please note that in the subject line.

-----

## Before You Contact Us

### Looking for crisis support?

If you’re in crisis, please visit our [Crisis & Safety](/crisis/index) section for immediate resources including hotlines by country.

### Looking for specific information?

Try using the search function or browsing our [section index](/home). The information you need may already be on the wiki.

### Want to report inaccurate information?

We take accuracy seriously. When reporting errors, please include:

- The page URL
- What’s incorrect
- The correct information (with source if possible)

-----

## A Note on Our Approach

DisabilityWiki centers disabled people’s expertise. We:

- Prioritize responses from disabled community members
- Welcome contributions from people with lived experience
- Value community knowledge alongside academic sources
- Use identity-first language as our default

We’re not a crisis service, legal advisor, or medical provider. We’re a community-built information resource.
