# Disabled Creators Directory

A directory of disabled artists, writers, musicians, filmmakers, performers, and creators across all media. This page helps you discover and support disabled creators.

---

## About This Directory

This directory collects disabled creators in one place for easy discovery. All creators listed here are openly disabled and create work that may or may not be disability-focused.

**This directory is always growing.** Suggest additions via [How to Contribute](/glossary/how-to-contribute).

**Legend:**
- 🌍 Non-US based

## How to Support Disabled Creators

### Direct Support

- **Buy their work** – Books, art, music, merchandise
- **Pay for content** – Patreon, subscriptions, tips
- **Hire them** – Speaking, consulting, commissions
- **Attend events** – Shows, readings, performances

### Amplification

- **Share their work** – Social media, recommendations
- **Credit properly** – Name them, link to their platforms
- **Review and recommend** – Books, shows, albums
- **Request accessibility** – Ask venues to book accessible acts

### Systemic Change

- **Advocate for funding** – Arts grants for disabled artists
- **Push for representation** – In media, publishing, galleries
- **Challenge gatekeeping** – Inaccessible venues, ableist institutions

## Contributing

To be added to this directory:

1. **You must be disabled** (self-identification accepted)
2. **You create publicly available work**
3. **Include:** Name, disability (if you share publicly), medium, notable work, website/social

To suggest other creators:

1. **They must be openly disabled**
2. **Include same information above**
3. **Verify they're comfortable being listed**
