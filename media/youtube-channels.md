# Disability YouTube Channels

Disabled content creators on YouTube covering lifestyle, advocacy, education, comedy, vlogs, and more. This list centers disabled creators telling their own stories.

---

## About This List

This list features **disabled YouTubers** creating content about disability experience, advocacy, daily life, and more. We prioritize creators who are open about their disabilities and create disability-related content.

**Legend:**
- 🎬 Disabled creator
- 🌍 Non-US based
- ✦ Highly recommended

**Note:** Subscriber counts change frequently. Categories reflect primary content focus.

## Highly Recommended

Start here for some of the most influential disabled YouTubers.

- ✦ 🎬 **Squirmy and Grubs** – Shane Burcaw (SMA) and Hannah Aylward. Couple sharing their interabled relationship with humor and honesty. Breaks down misconceptions about disability and relationships. 2M+ subscribers.

- ✦ 🎬 **Jessica Kellgren-Fozard** – UK-based Deaf, chronically ill, LGBTQ+ creator. Vintage fashion, disability education, queer history. Warm, informative, beautifully produced. 900K+ subscribers. 🌍

- ✦ 🎬 **Molly Burke** – Blind lifestyle and motivational content. Makeup tutorials, daily life, speaking career. One of the most visible blind creators. 2M+ subscribers.

- ✦ 🎬 **Zach Anner** – Comedian with cerebral palsy. Won Oprah's "Your OWN Show" competition. Travel, comedy, disability humor. 500K+ subscribers.

- ✦ 🎬 **Annie Elainey** – Disabled Latina creator covering disability justice, chronic illness, accessibility. Intersectional advocacy content.

- ✦ 🎬 **Rikki Poynter** – Deaf creator focusing on accessibility, captioning advocacy, Deaf awareness. Led #NoMoreCRAPtions campaign for better YouTube captions.

## Accessibility Note

Many disabled YouTubers prioritize accessibility:
- **Captions:** Look for CC icon; quality varies
- **Audio description:** Rare but growing
- **Transcripts:** Some creators provide in description or website

Support creators who provide accessible content!
