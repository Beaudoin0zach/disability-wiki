# Employment & Work

Work can be a source of income, structure, meaning—and also exploitation and harm.

This section covers:

- **Job Searching with a Disability** – Resumes, disclosure decisions, interviews, and job boards.
- **Workplace Accommodations** – Rights, practical strategies, and examples.
- **Employment Rights by Country** – Anti-discrimination laws and enforcement.
- **Supported Employment and Vocational Rehab** – Programs that are supposed to help, and how they often fall short.
- **Entrepreneurship and Self-Employment** – Running your own business or freelance work, including benefits and tax considerations.

We recognize that not everyone can or wants to work in waged employment, and that **rest and survival** are also valid priorities.
